6 Stage Order Delivery Process

  • Tagging - Clothes are tagged to avoid garment loss & Potential Mix up
  • Sorting & Inspection - based on garment, care label, fabric type & Color
  • Stain Treatment - Garments treated individually based on type of stain and care label.
  • Processing - Garments are carefully washed and cleaned by experienced professionals.
  • Finishing - Crisp press with steam iron to ensure crisp, clean garments ready to wear.
  • Quality Check - Last stage check before packing by our professionals.
  • Packing - Folded in Hanger or plastic packing as per preference.
